Dining plan — confirmed spots

On the 2026 plan: BackBeatQue, Farmhouse Kitchen & Grill, Grand Pavilion, Hugo's, Coasters Drive-In, Happy Friar, Panda Express, Frontier Inn, Miss Keat's, Corral, Derby Dogs. Entrée + side every 90 min. $35 plan = no drinks; $49 Premium adds drinks + snacks. Barrels & Bites tastings NOT on plan.

Locker rides (4)

Steel Vengeance, Maverick, Top Thrill 2, Siren's Curse: no loose articles, metal detectors, free lockers. Ziplock for phone/wallet/keys. ~5 min each visit.

Top Thrill 2 reality check

Operating in 2026, but ride it early, never save for last. If down, check every 30 min — often returns mid-afternoon. Monday 11am = built-in safety net.

Wind closures

TT2 closes first (lowest threshold), then Millennium Force, Valravn, GateKeeper, WindSeeker. Windy forecast = ride those the moment you can.

Fast Lane bail-out rule
