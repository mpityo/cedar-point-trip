Dining plan — your spots, confirmed

All on the 2026 plan: BackBeatQue, Farmhouse Kitchen & Grill, Grand Pavilion, Hugo's, Coasters Drive-In, Happy Friar, plus Panda Express, Frontier Inn, Miss Keat's, Corral, Derby Dogs. Entrée + side every 90 min. $35 All-Day plan has NO drinks — the $49 Premium adds drinks + snack redemptions (pretzels, popcorn, Happy Friar fries, ice cream). Barrels & Bites tastings are not on the plan.

Locker rides (4)

Steel Vengeance, Maverick, Top Thrill 2, Siren's Curse: no loose articles, metal detectors, free lockers at entrance. Bring a ziplock so phone + wallet + keys go in as one unit. Budget 5 min each visit.

Top Thrill 2 reality check

Operating in 2026, but its history says ride it early, never save it for last. If it's down, check the app every 30 min — it often returns mid-afternoon. Monday 11am is your built-in safety net.

Wind closures

Lake Erie wind closes rides: TT2 first (lowest threshold), then Millennium Force, Valravn, GateKeeper, WindSeeker. A windy forecast = ride those four the moment you can.

Fast Lane bail-out rule

If by 11am Saturday all four headliners (SteVe, Maverick, TT2, Siren's Curse) show 90+ min with no rain coming, buy single-day Fast Lane Plus in the app for the afternoon. Note: those four are Fast Lane Plus only — standard FL doesn't cover them.

Show options

Hoots & Hollers stand-up — Palace Theatre, Fri/Sat/Sun (9pm show 21+). New Jack Aldrich Theatre musical revue (Woodstock-era pop). Barrels & Bites live music on Frontier Trail from 4pm.
